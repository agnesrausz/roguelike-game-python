# Tech interview workbook

As you might already know, your Codecool year will end in a series
of trial interviews helping you to prepare for real life job
interviews. Our school focuses on practical skills in the first
place, but for a successful technical interview you need'll to
be prepared about theoretical questions as well.

The preparation for the technical interviews should be
an ongoing process. With the link below you'll get
a repository with interview questions grouped by modules.
Please keep answering these questions milestone by milestone
(select only those that are connected to the milestone).
This way you can keep your theoretical knowledge up-to-date
during the whole year and you don't have to learn from scratch
just before the tech interviews at the end of the year.

This workbook is introduced now in Budapest. Independently
from your location the questions of it are useful
for you to learn, but about the exact rules please ask your local mentors.

## Rules of usage

You *have to* answer all the questions connected to a module
in order to be allowed to start a PA. You are *advised to*
select and answer all the relevant questions whenever you
reach a milestone.

- You have to commit and push your changes regularly to GitHub.

You have to ask a fellow student to review your answers.
When you'll know what a pull request is, ask for a review
that way; until then just ask somebody to check your answers.
