### Disclaimer

Many of the following links cover much more topics than it is required for this competency. Don't read too much in
advance, and always try the new things out in practice!

### How to practice?

The best practice is to read through your (or your peers') existing pieces of code and look for code smells. The best if
you go on and *refactor* them right on the spot. Try to make them as clean as possible.
